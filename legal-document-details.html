KYC & AML Compliance Policy

Andro Markets LTD ensures strict KYC and AML compliance, preventing fraud, money laundering, and terrorism financing through identity verification and monitoring.

KYC / AML Policies

  • Andro Markets LTD is committed to maintaining the highest standards of compliance in the fight against money laundering (AML) and counter-terrorist financing (CTF). The company’s AML policy aims to actively prevent risks associated with illicit financial activities. To support the government’s efforts in combating terrorism financing and money laundering, financial institutions are legally required to obtain, verify, and record identifying information for each account holder. Andro Markets LTD is obligated to report any suspicious client activity related to money laundering.

Understanding Money Laundering

Money laundering is the process of converting funds derived from illegal activities (e.g., fraud, corruption, terrorism) into investments or assets that appear legitimate, thereby concealing their true origin.

The money laundering process consists of three stages:

  • Placement – Introducing illicit funds into the financial system through financial instruments such as bank accounts, money transfers, or high-value purchases. In some cases, multiple small deposits (smurfing) are made to avoid detection.

  • Layering –Moving funds between multiple accounts and financial instruments to obscure the transaction trail and hide the original source.

  • Integration – The funds are reintroduced into the economy through seemingly legitimate business transactions, investments, or asset purchases.

AML Compliance at Andro Markets LTD

Andro Markets LTD strictly adheres to AML principles to prevent the misuse of its services for illicit financial activities, including money laundering and terrorist financing. The company:

  • Does not accept or process cash transactions under any circumstances.

  • Reserves the right to suspend operations involving clients suspected of illegal activities.

  • Ensures compliance with all applicable laws and regulations issued by financial authorities.

Company Procedures

To comply with AML and KYC regulations, Andro Markets LTD follows these measures:

  • Know Your Customer (KYC) Policy and Due Diligence

    Before establishing any business relationship, Andro Markets LTD verifies the identity of all clients to ensure legitimacy. Additional scrutiny is applied to clients from high-risk jurisdictions identified by credible sources as having inadequate AML standards.

    For Individual Clients

    • Personal Information: Full name, date of birth, country of origin, and full residential address.

    • Identity Verification: One of the following

      • Valid passport (photo and signature must be visible)

      • Driver’s license with a photograph

      • National identity card (front and back)

    • Proof of Address:A utility bill or bank statement (not older than three months) confirming the client’s residential address.

    For Corporate Clients

    If a company is publicly listed, additional identity verification may not be required. Otherwise, the following documentation must be provided:

    • Certificate of incorporation or national equivalent

    • Memorandum and Articles of Association

    • Certificate of good standing or proof of registered address

    • Board resolution authorizing account opening and assigned operators

    • Copies of powers of attorney granted by the directors

    • Proof of identity for directors managing the account (as per individual client verification)

    • Proof of identity of ultimate beneficial owners or controlling individuals

  • Monitoring Customer Activity

    addition to collecting client information, Andro Markets LTD continuously monitors transactions to detect and prevent suspicious activity. Any transaction inconsistent with the client’s known business activities or transaction history may be flagged for further investigation.

    The company employs both automated and manual transaction monitoring systems to identify potential threats.

  • Record-Keeping

    Andro Markets LTD maintains records of all transaction data, identification documents, and other AML-related documentation (e.g., suspicious activity reports). These records are retained for at least seven years after an account is closed.

Enforcement and Reporting

If Andro Markets LTD suspects a client is engaging in money laundering or other illegal activities, it will:

  • Report the suspicious activity to the relevant regulatory authorities.

  • Temporarily block or permanently close the client’s account at its discretion.

  • Take all necessary legal actions in accordance with applicable regulations.
