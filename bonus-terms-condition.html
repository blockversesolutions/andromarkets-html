Campaign Period

1st July 2025 to 31st July 2025

1. Campaign Overview

AndroMarkets is pleased to launch its exclusive Tradable Bonus on Deposit Campaign. Clients who deposit during the campaign period are eligible to receive a 100% tradable bonus (up to $300) and a chance to win exciting lucky draw prizes.

2. Eligibility Criteria

  • The offer is available to all new and existing clients of AndroMarkets.

  • The minimum deposit required to qualify is $100.
  • Only one bonus per client is allowed (per verified profile/IP).
  • Only standard and micro accounts are eligible.
  • MT5 accounts must be fully verified with completed KYC.

3. Bonus Details

  • The bonus amount is 100% of the first deposit, capped at $300

  • Bonus funds are tradable only and cannot be withdrawn.

  • Profits earned from the bonus can be withdrawn once trading volume requirements are met.

  • The bonus is automatically removed if any withdrawal is made before completing the required trading volume.

4.Trading Volume Requirement

  • To unlock profit withdrawal: 3 standard lots must be traded per $100 bonus received.

  • For example: a $300 bonus requires 9 lots traded to withdraw profits.

5.Bonus Expiry

  • Bonus will remain active for 30 days from the deposit date.

  • If required trading volume is not met in 30 days, bonus and related profit may be canceled.
