How the global currency market works

The currency market is the largest investment market in the world. It is where national currencies, such as the US dollar (USD) or the Japanese yen (JPY), are exchanged.

  • Currencies are listed as pairs. They look like this: USDJPY 149.5190. Here, it means you can buy one lot, or 100,000 US dollars, for 14,951,900 yen.

  • Most private traders do not have enough funds to buy an entire lot. Octa allows you to trade 0.01 lots and use margin trading to profit from Forex pairs, even with small deposits.

  • To make a profit on the Forex market, you buy a pair and wait for its price to grow, then sell it. You can also sell a pair to repurchase it when the price decreases.

How to trade Forex currency pairs

  • For novices in Forex trading, the best choice is major pairs: EURUSD, GBPUSD, and USDJPY. They are relatively stable, liquid, and overall more predictable than others.

  • You can benefit from any movements of the market. All you need is to predict how the price will change. There are two major approaches to that: fundamental and technical.

  • Fundamental methods focus on external factors: events, statements, global trends, or interest rates. News is a particularly valuable source of trading ideas here.
