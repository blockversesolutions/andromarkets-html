Funds Security

Funds security refers to measures that protect your investments from theft, fraud, or loss, ensuring financial assets remain safe and accessible.

Join the market

Segregated Accounts

In accordance with international regulation standards, we use separate accounts to keep our traders' funds segregated from the company's balance sheets. This keeps your funds secure and untouched.

Segregated accounts are separate accounts where clients' funds are held apart from a company’s operational funds, providing enhanced security and protection, ensuring that client assets are not used for business operations.

SSL-Protected Personal Are..

We use highly secure technology to protect your personal data and financial transactions. SSL-secured profile will keep your data safe and inaccessible to any third parties thanks to strong encryption.

An SSL-protected personal area ensures that your sensitive information is encrypted during transmission, safeguarding your privacy and data integrity while using online platforms for financial transactions or personal management.

Account Verification

We recommend you verify your account by submitting a scan of your personal ID and address proof. This measure will make sure your transactions are authorised and secure.

Account verification is the process of confirming a user's identity and legitimacy, typically involving documentation or personal information, to enhance security and prevent fraud in online transactions and services.

Secure Withdrawal Rules

Since withdrawal from a real account becomes available only after email confirmation, only you can access your funds. It is also required that you use the same payment details for deposits and withdrawals. Thus, under no circumstances can we transfer your withdrawal to an unauthorised third party.

Secure withdrawal rules govern the process for safely extracting funds, ensuring user identity verification and protecting against unauthorized access and fraud.

3D Secure Visa Authentication

We apply the 3D Secure technology when processing credit and debit cards. This technology makes all Visa transactions transparent and safe.

3D Secure Visa authentication is an added layer of security for online transactions, requiring cardholders to verify their identity through a password or unique code, reducing fraud and ensuring safer purchases.

Advanced Protection

Our technical environment is monitored 24/7 by a dedicated team of professional security engineers and technical specialists. They have developed and maintain top level protection, so any data loss, damage, or other technical issues are highly unlikely.

Advanced protection encompasses comprehensive security measures designed to safeguard personal information and financial assets against emerging threats and cyber vulnerabilities effectively.
